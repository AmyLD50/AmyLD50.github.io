About Me

Hi, I'm Amy! I'm 19 years old, and as a trans woman navigating the world of technology, I've found my place in Computer Information Systems, focusing on cybersecurity. From a young age, I was fascinated by how things worked behind the screen. I remember picking up my first programming book, a guide to JavaScript, and realizing that this was something I truly loved. This passion led me to delve into backend development with Node.js, where I learned how to create server-side applications and APIs.

As I grew, my curiosity only deepened. Computers became more than just machines; they were canvases for creativity and problem-solving. Today, I’m channeling that curiosity into a formal education in cybersecurity. I’m exploring the fascinating—and sometimes dangerous—world of digital security, aiming to understand vulnerabilities and protect information. My path has also taken a creative turn, as I'm now working on a game with friends, merging my love for tech with storytelling, music, and more.
